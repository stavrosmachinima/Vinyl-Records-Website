A few words about us

We are a group of music lovers. We grew up with 20th century rock and jazz. At the beginning of the last decade we decided to make our hobby career and start operating dynamically our record store. We have a variety of different products for all your vinyl needs. We always try our best to offer the highest quality goods at the most competitive prices.

Our mission is simple: to provide the best products and services to our customers at the lowest possible prices. We are very proud of our company, our commitment to customer service and the products we sell. Our online store is designed to provide you with a safe and secure environment to browse our products in our online catalog. We hope you enjoy your stay in our online store!


You will find the following records in our store:
  • Bohemian Rhapsody by Queen (1975)
  • The Wall by Pink FLoyd (1979)
  • Mothership by Led Zeppelin (2007)
